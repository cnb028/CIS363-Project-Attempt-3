About Me


Since 2022, Christine Bettencourt has been the owner and operator of Bouncing Bunny Studio, a game development company with plans for a Virtual Reality pet simulator game.

Currently Christine lives in Oklahoma City with her partner and their three dogs.

While originaly working in the field of Criminal Justice as a Correctional Officer for the Texas Department of Criminal Justice, Christine discovered a love of programming.

When not writing code, Christine enjoys video games, reading, and spending time with her dogs and partner out of the house. She also really enjoys listening to history or literature podcasts.
